Unlike most industries, healthcare providers have been slow to move their infrastructure to the cloud.

Here are five reasons to consider setting up or moving your imaging
infrastructure to the cloud

An in-premise solution

  • Images not easily retrievable
  • Cost increases per user
  • Device based software restricted to use at centre
  • High cost of IT maintenance
    • Redundant storage cost for physical records

Cloud-based platform – Dekco

  • Instant access to patient documents
  • Unlimited users at fixed cost
  • Scalability - can be used at unlimited centres
  • Maintenance handled by Dekco
    • Eliminate storage costs
